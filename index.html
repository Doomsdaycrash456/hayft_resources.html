Mental Health Resources for Students

Below are some helpful mental health resources for students of all ages:

  • MentalHealth.gov - Official U.S. Government site with resources for mental health support.
  • Kids Help Phone - Confidential online and telephone counseling and volunteer-led, text-based support in English and French.
  • Canadian Mental Health Association - Resources and support for Canadians struggling with mental health.
  • National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) - Resources for people affected by mental health conditions in the U.S.
  • BounceBack - A Canadian program for helping people manage mild to moderate depression, stress, or anxiety.
  • TeenMentalHealth.org - Resources designed specifically for teens about mental health and well-being.
  • Wellness Foundation - A foundation supporting youth mental health and emotional well-being.
  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline - A critical resource for anyone in crisis or contemplating suicide.
  • Calm - A meditation and mental health app offering tools for managing stress and anxiety.
  • Headspace - A meditation app to help reduce stress, improve sleep, and manage anxiety.
